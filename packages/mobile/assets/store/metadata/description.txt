Modulo Squares turns divisibility into a fast, strategic arcade puzzle.

Guide each falling numbered square into the right bucket before time runs out. A clean division grows your score and combo. A remainder costs points. One dead bucket can punish a careless move, so read the board, move quickly, and plan ahead.

HOW TO PLAY
• Move the falling number left or right across ten lanes
• Land on a bucket that divides the number evenly
• Build combos to increase your movement speed
• Fill the progress grid to advance to the next level
• Adapt as the number range expands and the drop speed increases

FEATURES
• Quick, replayable falling-number gameplay
• Optional visual cues for learning divisibility patterns
• Persistent local high score across runs
• Sign in with Apple, Google, or email
• Free, ad-supported play
• One-time Unlock Premium purchase (removes ads) with purchase restoration
• Account deletion from in-app Settings

Modulo Squares is designed for puzzle fans, competitive players, and anyone who wants arithmetic practice to feel like a game instead of a worksheet.
