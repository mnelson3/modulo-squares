# Modulo Squares - Full Description

Challenge your mind with Modulo Squares, a unique puzzle game that combines strategy, logic, and mathematical thinking!

**Core Gameplay:**
- Navigate a 4x4 grid filled with numbers
- Use modulo operations to clear tiles strategically
- Progress through increasingly challenging levels
- Master special tiles that add exciting twists

**Key Features:**
- ✅ Intuitive tap-to-move and swipe controls
- ✅ Level-based progression system
- ✅ Special tiles with unique effects
- ✅ Global leaderboard competition
- ✅ Cross-platform play (iOS, Android, Web)
- ✅ Ad-supported free-to-play experience

**How to Play:**
1. Select a numbered tile on the grid
2. Move it to an adjacent empty space or occupied tile
3. When moving to an occupied tile, apply modulo operation
4. Clear all numbers to complete the level
5. Unlock new challenges and climb the leaderboard!

**Perfect for:**
- Puzzle game enthusiasts
- Math lovers and logic thinkers
- Casual gamers seeking brain-teasing challenges
- Players who enjoy strategic gameplay

**Why Modulo Squares?**
Unlike traditional match-3 or tile-sliding games, Modulo Squares introduces mathematical strategy through modulo operations. Each move requires careful calculation and planning, creating a truly unique gaming experience that rewards logical thinking and pattern recognition.
